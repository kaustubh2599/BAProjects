Which economies in Asia grew the most in relative terms ?¶

Defining growth in terms of incremental evolution of GDP and life expectancy¶

In [31]:
UniYear = pd.DataFrame(index=AsianEco.year.unique())

for count in (AsianEco.country.unique()):
    df = AsianEco[AsianEco['country']==count]
    df = (df.drop(['country','continent'],axis = 1).sort_values(by='year'))
    df[count] = df['pop']*df['gdpPercap']
    
    df.set_index('year',inplace = True)
    init_Val = df[count].iloc[0]
    df_GDP =  df[count]/init_Val * 100

    UniYear = UniYear.join(df_GDP)

Yes, there is relationship between GDP per Capita and Life Expectancy as GDP increases Life Expectancy also increase. We can also see there that there almost none of the country with gdpPercap more than the 20000 has life expactancy less than 40 years.
